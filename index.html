Importance of Animals

Every animal within the ecosystem whether big or small has a vital role in the well-being of the planet. If even one species become extinct, it can have significant effects throughout the rest of the food chain. For example even a small bee helps the plants to pollinate which provides us with food and other necessary things. And not only animals help the environment but they also help us. They help us in many different ways in our day to day life like lifting wieght and agriculture. Many Domestic Animals provides food to us and help us. Practical uses/importance of animals

  1. For clothing and textiles
  2. For work and transport
  3. In medicine
  4. In hunting
  5. As pets

Endangered Animals

Today, due to human activities more and more animals are becoming endangered and in some cases there are not even a hundred of some species are left. Many species became endangered because of losing their habitats and many because they were hunted for their vulnarable parts and food. Today there are more than 40,000 species endangered and 16,000 are threatened with extinction. To keep these animals safe the government made wildlife santuries and nation parks which helped in the increase of their population.

Extinct Animals

Many endangered animals became extinct in the past years and many are becoming extinct today also but why? This is because of a number of reasons like global warming, humans hunting them and lost of habitats etc. When animals become extinct an imbalance in the food chain occur and might lead to extinction of other animals who eat them.
