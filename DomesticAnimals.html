Domestic Animals

The animals which live close to us and help us are called Domestic Animals. These include our pets,farm animals and others. Lets know about some of them...

Cows:
One of the most helpful Domestic animals which probides us with dairy products. They also help us in farming etc.

Goats:
These are also one of the most helpful animals and provides us with milk,meat etc.

Sheep:
These helpful creature provides us with fibre,meat, and milk etc.
